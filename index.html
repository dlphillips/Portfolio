About Me

I am originally from Louisville, Kentucky and relocated to Charlotte in 2014. My undergraduate education was fueled by interests in weather and cartography. This led to an Associates Degree in Meteorology from Western Kentucky University and was followed by a Bachelors Degree in Geography from the University of Louisville. There, I focused my studies on an emerging computer technology: Geographic Information Systems.

My career thus far has been very fulfilling. Automating complex procedures through either database or GIS technologies is what I have found most rewarding. My current employer is a provider of wall maps and geographic information for restaurants and point of sale systems throught the US & Canada. Processes to automate map creation and data formatting were authored by myself and fellow team members.

Throughout my career, I have always liked helping others make well-informed decisions through clear, engaging presentation of data. I see web development as a natural progression that will allow me to provide similar insights in new, innovative ways.

On the personal side, my free time is spent with family and serving in my church. I was a volunteer leader for the Boy Scouts of America in Louisville and I am proud to say I completed Wood Badge training; the most advanced leadership training course offered by the Boy Scouts of America.
